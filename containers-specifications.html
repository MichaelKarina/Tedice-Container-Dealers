Additionally, there are specialized containers, such as high-cube containers with extra height, open-top containers for oversized cargo, and refrigerated containers for temperature-sensitive items, catering to the diverse needs of the shipping industry.

These standardized shipping container sizes have revolutionized global trade by simplifying cargo handling and ensuring efficient use of space on ships, trains, and trucks. They have become the backbone of international shipping, allowing for seamless transfers between different modes of transportation and reducing the need for extensive cargo handling.

The versatility and uniformity of shipping containers have made them a cornerstone of the modern supply chain, enabling goods to traverse the globe in a cost-effective and organized manner. Whether you are shipping a small batch of electronics or large industrial equipment, the wide array of shipping container sizes ensures that there’s an appropriate solution for transporting goods safely and efficiently to virtually any destination worldwide.

Benefits of Shipping Containers

  • Instant: Shipping Containers offer an instant storage space which can be delivered and dropped into place with no construction and minimal site preparation.
  • Secure: The containers have secure double lock doors with thick steel panels enabling a secure storage space.
  • Modular: As your storage requirements grow, further containers can merely be added and can even be placed on top of each other to allow for better floor space use.
  • Mobile: If your business moves, your container can move with you.
