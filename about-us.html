About Us

Welcome to Tedice Container Dealers – Kenya’s trusted source for quality used shipping containers. Tedice was incorporated in 2007 to offer Logistical Services and supply of new and used shipping containers for sale together with container consultancy and conversion services.

Founded with a passion for delivering affordable, reliable, and versatile space solutions, we specialize in the sale and modification of used shipping containers. Whether you're looking for a simple storage unit, a custom office, retail shop, classroom, or a creative build, we have the right container for you.

Our Mission

To provide affordable, high-quality container solutions that meet the evolving space and storage needs of individuals, businesses, and institutions across Kenya.

What We Do

  • Sell used 20ft and 40ft shipping containers
  • Modify containers into offices, shops, classrooms, and homes
  • Deliver and install containers anywhere in Kenya

Whether you wish to renovate your shipping container into a retail space or cafe, build a new family home, transport goods, or simply use the extra space on your property, our shipping containers can be utilized for pretty much anything.

With years of experience and a growing list of satisfied clients, we take pride in our reputation for honesty, unbeatable prices, and timely delivery.
